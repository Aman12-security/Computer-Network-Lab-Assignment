Sliding Window Simulation Results:

- Multiple frames transmitted without waiting for ACK
- Sliding window movement observed during transmission
- Acknowledgments received successfully
- Piggybacking observed (ACK included in outgoing frames)

Performance:

- Improved throughput compared to Stop-and-Wait
- Reduced number of acknowledgment packets
- Efficient utilization of network bandwidth

Conclusion:

Sliding Window with Piggybacking enhances performance by combining flow control and acknowledgment optimization.
